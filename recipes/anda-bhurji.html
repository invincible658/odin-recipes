Anda Bhurji

Egg bhurji, also known as Anda Bhurji or Ande ka Khagina, is a scrambled eggs dish which is a popular street food and a breakfast, lunch or dinner recipe. Bhurji can be found at highway rest stops and street food stalls across India, sometimes served alongside slices of white bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 cup Onions(chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Capsicum(chopped)
  • 1/3 cup Tomato(chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger(grated)
  • 1 Green chilly(chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspooon Cumin Seeds
  • 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilly Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garam Masala Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  • 2-3 tablespoon Cooking Oil
  • 1-1.5 teaspoon Salt or as required
  • 1 teaspoon Mint Leaves(chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander Leaves(chopped)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan , when it's hot enough ,add cumin seeds.
  2. Once cumin seeds is about to change colour , add chopped ginger , garlic and green chillies.
  3. Add chopped onions and saute well till it turns translucent.
  4. Now add in finely chopped capsicum and saute for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add in chopped tomatoes and cook till it turns soft. To speed up the process of cooking tomatoes, add ½ teaspoon salt.
  6. Add turmeric powder ,red chilli powder , coriander powder , garam masala or pav bhaji masala (refer notes) and mix well.
  7. Break in 4- 5 eggs into the above cooked vegetables and gently mix .
  8. Allow eggs to cook completely. Once its cooked ,scramble the eggs well to mix along with vegetables.
  9. Now add ½ teaspoon of black pepper powder , ½ -¾ teaspoon of salt and mix well.
  10. Garnish with mint and coriander leaves.
  11. Serve it as a side dish along with Toasted Bread, Roti or Rice-Sambar.
